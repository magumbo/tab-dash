How to set up your mobile domain

  1. Add the custom `m.yoursite.com` domain on the left
  2. Visit your domain providers website (where you purchased your domain). In many instances, this will be godaddy.com
  3. Log in and access your account settings page
  4. On the Domain Details page, locate and click on the launch link under the DNS Manager section
  5. In the Zone File Editor page go to the CNAME (ALIAS) section and enter `m` as host and point this to {{activeSite.Default_Mobile_Domain__c}}.{{locals['slugBaseDomain']}}
  6. Save Changes
